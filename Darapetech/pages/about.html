Our Story

Where It All Began

Darapet Technology started with a simple belief: that small businesses and independent entrepreneurs deserve access to the same quality of digital services as Fortune 500 companies — without the agency overheads or inflated fees.

What began as a one-person freelance operation has grown into a full creative & technology agency, delivering premium results across web development, design, video, and marketing. Every project is treated as if it were our own.

Today, Darapet serves clients globally — from solo founders launching their first product to established businesses scaling their digital presence. Our promise remains unchanged: real premium quality, every single time.
