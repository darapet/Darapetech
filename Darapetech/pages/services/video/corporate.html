How It Works

Our Process

A clear, structured approach so you always know what's happening next.

01

Brief & Brand Review

We review your brand guidelines, reference videos, key messages, and intended use before editing begins.

02

Footage Review

We review all raw footage, select the strongest takes, and map the editorial structure.

03

First Assembly

Rough cut delivered for you to review the overall structure and pace before polishing begins.

04

Full Edit

Full edit with colour grade, graphics, music, and all brand elements integrated and refined.

05

Approval & Revisions

Review with your team, collect feedback, and apply all changes within 48 hours.

06

Final Delivery

Master file plus platform-specific exports delivered with chapter markers and full naming convention.
