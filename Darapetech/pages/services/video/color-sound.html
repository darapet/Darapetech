What We Deliver

8 Ways We Make Your Colour Grading & Sound Design Outstanding

Every item on this list is something we actually deliver — not a promise, not a maybe.

Full Colour Grade

Primary and secondary colour correction, shot matching across angles and locations, LUT application, and a signature look developed for your specific content and distribution target.

Colour Correction

Exposure correction, white balance normalisation, skin tone correction, and shadow/highlight recovery — making every shot look technically correct before the creative grade begins.

Shot Matching

Multi-camera footage, different days, or different cameras matched to look consistent — so the edit flows invisibly and no shot looks out of place.

Cinematic Look Development

Custom LUTs and node stacks developed for your content — warm and golden, cool and clinical, or moody and desaturated — a look that becomes recognisably yours.

Dialogue Enhancement

Noise reduction, EQ shaping, de-essing, compression, and reverb matching to make every voice track sound clear, natural, and professional regardless of recording conditions.

Music Scoring & Selection

Background score selected and mixed to sit naturally beneath dialogue — not competing, not drowning — timed to the emotional beats of the content.

Sound Effects & Foley

Ambient sound design, transition whooshes, UI sounds, impact effects, and environmental audio layered to add depth and cinematic quality to the sound experience.

Loudness Mastering

Final audio mix mastered to platform-specific loudness targets — YouTube (-14 LUFS), broadcast (-23 LUFS), or podcast standard (-16 LUFS) — delivered broadcast-ready.

How It Works

Our Process

A clear, structured approach so you always know what's happening next.

01

Send Project Files

Your edited video timeline exported as XML/AAF/EDL, or the rendered video file if working without the original project.

02

Technical Review

We assess footage quality, dynamic range, and audio tracks to plan the correction and grade approach.

03

Primary Correction

All shots corrected for exposure, white balance, and technical accuracy before any creative work begins.

04

Creative Grade

Signature look applied across all footage with shot-matching reviewed on a calibrated monitor.

05

Sound Design

Audio tracks processed, music added, sound effects layered, and final mix levelled to target loudness.

06

Delivery

Graded and mixed video exported in your required format plus stems delivered separately if needed.
