What We Deliver

8 Ways We Make Your Video Ads Editing Outstanding

Every item on this list is something we actually deliver — not a promise, not a maybe.

Platform-Specific Hook Editing

The first 1–3 seconds make or break an ad. We engineer every video opening as a pattern interrupt — a question, a bold statement, a surprising visual, or a problem statement that stops the thumb.

Benefit-Focused Messaging

Your product's value presented clearly and quickly — benefits, not features — with visual evidence, testimonial clips, or product demonstrations supporting every claim.

Strong Call-to-Action Design

Animated CTA overlays, end cards, and voiceover prompts telling viewers exactly what to do next — optimised for the platform's ad placement and audience intent.

Multiple Ad Variants

We edit multiple versions from the same footage — different hooks, different CTAs, different lengths — so you can A/B test creatives and scale the winners.

Multi-Format Delivery

Square (1:1), vertical (9:16), horizontal (16:9), and story (4:5) formats all delivered — correct for every ad placement on every platform without cropping your subject off-screen.

Captions for Silent Viewing

85% of social video is watched without sound. Bold, accurate captions ensure your ad's message lands even in the scroll with audio off.

Brand-Consistent Treatment

Colour grade, fonts, and visual style consistent with your brand — so every ad feels like it belongs to the same campaign and reinforces brand recognition.

Performance-Informed Editing

We study high-performing ads in your industry before editing. Ad structure, pacing, and hook style informed by what actually converts in your category.

How It Works

Our Process

A clear, structured approach so you always know what's happening next.

01

Ad Brief

Provide your product footage, any existing creatives, target audience, and the desired CTA.

02

Hook Selection

We identify the strongest hook angles from your footage and outline the script structure.

03

Edit Multiple Variants

3–5 ad variants edited with different hooks and CTAs from the same footage for A/B testing.

04

Captions & Graphics

Captions, CTA overlays, and brand elements added to all variants.

05

Review

Review all variants — feedback applied within 24 hours.

06

Multi-Format Delivery

All variants delivered in all required platform formats, named and ready to upload to Ads Manager.
