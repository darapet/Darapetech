What We Deliver

8 Ways We Make Your Podcast Video Editing Outstanding

Every item on this list is something we actually deliver — not a promise, not a maybe.

Multi-Camera Podcast Edit

Seamless multi-cam editing that switches between angles at the right moments — reaction shots, B-roll cutaways, and speaker closeups to keep the visual experience engaging throughout.

Professional Audio Enhancement

Noise reduction, EQ, compression, and de-essing applied to every track — your podcast sounds like a professional studio production even if recorded in a home office.

Content Editing & Cleanup

Long pauses, filler words, tangents, and technical errors removed — tightening the episode without losing the natural conversational feel.

Chapter Markers & Timestamps

Timestamped chapters formatted for YouTube, Spotify, and Apple Podcasts — making your content searchable and easy to navigate for listeners.

Short Clip Repurposing

The best 3–5 moments from each episode cut into standalone short-form clips ready for TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and LinkedIn.

Lower Thirds & Branding

Animated guest name lower thirds, show logo watermark, and branded episode title cards that make your podcast look polished and professional.

Captions & Transcription

Accurate captions synced to both the full episode and all short clips — increasing reach for deaf audiences and improving watch time on muted autoplay feeds.

Episode Thumbnail Creation

Custom episode thumbnails featuring guest headshots, bold title text, and your podcast brand — designed to drive clicks across YouTube and podcast directories.

How It Works

Our Process

A clear, structured approach so you always know what's happening next.

01

Upload Raw Files

Video files and audio tracks uploaded to our shared folder with the episode run sheet or topic notes.

02

Audio Edit

All audio tracks mixed, cleaned, and mastered to podcast-ready loudness standards (-16 LUFS).

03

Video Edit

Multi-cam edit assembled with graphics, lower thirds, intro/outro, and all branding elements.

04

Short Clips

Best moments identified and cut into short-form clips formatted for each platform.

05

Review

Full episode and clips delivered for your review — feedback applied within 24 hours.

06

Delivery

Final video, audio-only export, short clips, thumbnail, and timestamps delivered in one package.
