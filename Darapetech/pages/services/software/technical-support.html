How It Works

Our Process

A clear, structured approach so you always know what's happening next.

01

Issue Description

You tell us what's happening — via WhatsApp, email, or a quick call. We ask the right questions to understand the problem.

02

Remote Session

We connect via TeamViewer, AnyDesk, or Zoom screen share and diagnose the issue live on your machine.

03

Fix & Test

Issue resolved and tested to confirm it's fully working — not just appearing to work.

04

Root Cause Explanation

We explain what caused the problem in plain language so you understand it, not just that it's fixed.

05

Prevention Advice

Recommendations to prevent the issue recurring — software updates, configuration changes, or better practices.

06

Session Report

Brief summary of what was done sent to you by email — useful for your records or sharing with your team.
