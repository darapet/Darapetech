What We Deliver

8 Ways We Make Your Chatbot & AI Integration Outstanding

Every item on this list is something we actually deliver — not a promise, not a maybe.

Custom AI Chatbot Development

Context-aware chatbots for customer support, lead generation, or internal knowledge bases — trained on your data, responding in your brand's voice, handling complex multi-turn conversations.

RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation)

Connect your documents, PDFs, knowledge base, or database to an LLM so the AI answers questions using your actual business content — accurately, with source citations.

OpenAI & Anthropic API Integration

GPT-4o, Claude 3.5, Gemini Pro — whichever model fits your use case and budget, integrated into your product with proper prompt engineering and response handling.

Multi-Channel Deployment

Deploy your chatbot to your website, WhatsApp Business, Telegram, Slack, Discord, or any messaging platform — one AI brain, multiple touchpoints.

Document Processing & Extraction

Upload PDFs, Word docs, or spreadsheets — AI extracts, summarises, categorises, or answers questions from document content automatically.

AI Workflow Automation

LLM-powered workflows that classify incoming emails, draft responses, summarise meetings, tag support tickets, or score leads — replacing hours of manual work.

Guardrails & Safety Controls

System prompts, topic restrictions, content filtering, hallucination reduction, and confidence thresholds — your AI stays on-topic and doesn't go off-brand.

Conversation Analytics

Every conversation logged, analysed for common questions and failure patterns, and used to continuously improve your AI's accuracy and helpfulness.

How It Works

Our Process

A clear, structured approach so you always know what's happening next.

01

Use Case Definition

We map exactly what the AI should do, what data it needs, and what success looks like — before touching any API.

02

Data Preparation

Your documents, knowledge base, and content processed, chunked, and embedded into a vector database for retrieval.

03

Prompt Engineering

System prompts, few-shot examples, and retrieval logic engineered to give accurate, on-brand responses.

04

Integration Build

API integration, UI, and backend built — the AI connected to your existing systems and data sources.

05

Testing & Red-Teaming

Adversarial testing, edge case testing, and accuracy evaluation before any user sees the AI.

06

Deploy & Monitor

Production deployment with conversation logging, failure alerting, and a monthly accuracy review process.
